WHO WE ARE

We are a family business that has been running a restaurant in the middle of Vienna for 30 years.
Because of our origins, we have learned to love Bosnian cuisine.
Our aim was to offer our Viennese the same quality of food that we have come to appreciate for ourselves.
Our regular guests come to us regularly and have always given us the best recommendations.



WHY BOSNIAN FOOD

Bosnian cuisine is one of the healthiest cuisines in the world.
A lot of the cooking is done with vegetables and, if the wallet allows,
with some meat. In addition, a lot of fresh cheese is produced in Bosnia,
which is then used in various dishes.Pita is a dough dish which is prepared
with cheese, spinach, potatoes and meat and tastes very delicious



OUR PRODUCTS

Our products are not only known in Vienna but also throughout Austria.
Many visitors, especially Bosnians, come to us from Linz, Salzburg and
Graz over the weekend. They look forward to Ćevapćići, zeljanica,
Begova čorba and many other of our products. We buy our ingredients exclusively
organic and the meat halal.
